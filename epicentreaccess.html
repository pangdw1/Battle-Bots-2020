EPICentre Access

 

As you know making something with metal requires tools and machinery.  We as a team went over to the engineering school to ask if they had any offcut material for us to use.  William Early was kind enough to not only give us some material but take us on a guided tour through the centre.  

William suggested we go through the process of completing an induction to use the resources available.

As the EPICentre has all the tools and machinery available,  this would benefit us as a team to get 'Farmbot V2' Battlebot built faster.    

Our team will gain some knowledge on tools and machinery in a workshop environment. 



Certificate Of Completion

 Our team completed the EPICentre Induction to use the machinery at the Engineering School (Otago Polytechnic)
