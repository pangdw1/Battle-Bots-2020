EPICentre Access

 

Our team went over to the engineering school to ask if they had any off-cut material for us to use.  William Early was kind enough to not only give us some material, but take us on a guided tour through the centre.  

William suggested we go through the process of completing an induction to use the resources available.  After a few emails, we all had personal links to complete the induction.

As the EPICentre has all the tools and machinery available,  this would benefit us as a team to get 'Farmbot V2' Battlebot built faster.    

Our team gained some knowledge on tools and machinery in a workshop environment. 



Certificate Of Completion

 Our team completed the EPICentre Induction to use the machinery at the Engineering School (Otago Polytechnic)
